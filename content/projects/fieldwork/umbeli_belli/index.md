### My Role
**Field and Lab Assistant**  
- Excavation: Stratigraphic excavation and detailed field recording within a Middle Stone Age rock shelter context.
- Analytical recovery methods: Applied systematic sieving techniques to isolate and retain small lithic materials for specialist analysis.
- Material processing proficiency: Performed meticulous washing, labelling, and organisation of artefacts to maintain accurate provenance and documentation.
- Collections management skills: Contributed to structured cataloguing and careful packing of diverse archaeological materials to support long‑term curation and research integrity.
