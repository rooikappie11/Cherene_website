### My Role
**Student Field Assistant**  
- Field excavation: Participated in controlled excavation and systematic documentation of an Iron Age settlement.
- Archaeological recording skills: Supported accurate record‑keeping, photographic documentation, and contextual noting of features and finds.
- Collections handling ability: Assisted with cataloguing and cleaning artefacts to maintain clear provenance and organised site records.
