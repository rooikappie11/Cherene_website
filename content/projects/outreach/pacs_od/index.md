## Outreach Activity
As a member of the [Southern African Archaeology Student Council](https://www.saassarchaeology.com/), I volunteered at the Pretoria Archaeology Club for Schools (PACS) Open Day. The event was hosted at Mamelodi High School in Pretoria, South Africa, and introduced learners to hands‑on archaeological activities, demonstrations, and discussions designed to spark interest in the discipline.

### Date
May 2019
